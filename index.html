Likes and Hobbies

When I'm not coding or at Galvanize, you can typically find me outdoors or playing videogames. I love Colorado and all it has to offer. If I have enough time I like to take extended backpacking trips into the mountains. For me, getting outside is a great place to ground myself. It allows me to slow down and put my problems and life into prospective. Indoors, I love to play RPG adventure games while listening to classic rock artists such as Led Zeppelin, CCR, Steve Miller, and much more. When I need to wind down and watch something I love watching Sci-Fi classics like Star Wars (origional trillogy) or fantasy masterpieces such as The Lord of The Rings.

Etienne De Grellet

The quote I chose for my home page comes from a quaker by the name of Etienne De Grellet. This sentence is a mantra that I repeat to myself often. Not only does it remind me to treat people with humility, respect, and empathy, it also is a way of being. It is a sort of Carpe Diem for me. I will only live this day, this walk of life one time so I must do my utmost best to act in the present. Not only act in the present, but act in a good manner for my future self and the betterment of my piers.
